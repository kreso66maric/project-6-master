Game Rules

  • Players can move up to 3 spaces per turn
  • Landing on a weapon will pick it up and drop the old one.

  • Potion drink heals player by 15 points
  • When players cross paths, battle begins.
  • Player on turn attacks first.
  • At the end of turn a player can choose to defend until next turn.
